# Code Of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors should communicate respectfully, assume good intent, and keep discussions focused on improving the project.

Examples of expected behavior:

- being constructive in technical disagreement
- welcoming contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels
- keeping feedback specific, factual, and non-personal

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- publishing someone else's private information without consent
- abusive or discriminatory language

## Scope

This applies in the repository, issue tracker, pull requests, and project discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation when conduct repeatedly violates these standards.
